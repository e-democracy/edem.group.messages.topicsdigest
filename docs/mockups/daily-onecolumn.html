This is a mockup of the new E-Democracy Topics Digest. Please take a look and send your feedback to bill.bushey@e-democracy.org. When sending feedback, please include what you used to visit this page (i.e. a laptop, a phone, a tablet), what operating system (i.e. Windows, OS X, Android, iOS), and what browser (i.e. IE, Firefox, Safari, Dolphin).

Note that buttons to clips and to the top, as well as email links, work. All other buttons and links are dummies.

Are you a designer? If you're a designer in the Twin Cities, we invite you to send us your improved version of this digest. Just email bill.bushey@e-democracy.org with your HTML attached, or a URL. If we like your design we'll use it, credit you, and take you out to lunch!
